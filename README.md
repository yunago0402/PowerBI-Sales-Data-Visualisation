## Project Overview

This project intricately analyses the sales data across multiple years (2014-2017), focusing on key metrics like sales quantity, total sales, total profit, and customer behaviour. Using various data visualization techniques, the project aims to reveal insights into sales and profit trends, customer preferences, regional dynamics, and product performance. This analysis is essential for guiding strategic decisions, optimising marketing and sales efforts, and identifying opportunities for growth in North America, Pacific and Europe and product categories.

---

## Key Features

- **Unlocking Business Insights for Strategic Decision-Making:** This project offers a comprehensive analysis of sales data (2014-2017) to provide actionable insights for stakeholders looking to optimise sales strategies, understand market trends, and make data-driven decisions.
- **DAX Calculations for Custom Metrics:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) are used to compute essential business metrics such as total sales, total profit, and sales quantity across different time periods (calendar year and financial year).
Custom calculations enable in-depth analysis of factors such as sales by category, profit by region, and customer preferences, empowering stakeholders to assess profitability and performance from multiple angles.
- **Data Cleaning and Transformation:** A rigorous data cleaning process ensures that all sales data is consistent, accurate, and free of errors.
- **Ineteractive Visualizations for Deeper Exploration:** These interactive elements support trend analysis, scenario modeling, and allow users to understand the relationships between different sales factors (e.g., sales and profit, product performance, or regional differences).

---

## Highlights

- **Executive Summary:** A concise overview of the key findings and insights from the sales data analysis (2014–2017), offering essential information for decision-makers. This summary highlights opportunities to optimise business strategies, product offerings, and regional focus.
- **Sales and Profit Analysis:** Dive into detailed analysis of sales and profit trends across the years, with a special focus on 2016, which recorded the highest performance. This analysis offers actionable insights into the key drivers of sales and profitability across different product categories and regions.
- **Customer Behaviour Insights:** Examine customer buying patterns based on marital status, gender, and occupation. Insights include how married customers show higher purchasing demand and how males have a preference for road bikes, while females lean towards mountain and touring bikes.
- **Product and Category Performance:** Gain insights into the performance of various product categories, such as bikes, accessories, and clothing. Bikes consistently outperformed other categories, offering valuable insights into product pricing and positioning strategies.
- **Regional Sales and Profit Insights:** The regional breakdown highlights the dominant performance of North America, which consistently led in both sales and profit. The analysis also examines trends across Europe and the Pacific, offering strategic insights to help prioritise markets with growth potential.
